Project:
Indexation Reporting System

Problem:
In debt collection scenarios, overpayments or specific legal conditions may entitle debtors to claim indexation adjustments.
Without proper monitoring, this creates financial and legal risks.

Solution:

Developed a SQL-based reporting system to track cases eligible for indexation
Implemented logic to identify overpayments and relevant legal conditions
Structured outputs for monitoring and control of indexation processes

Impact:

Reduced financial risk
Improved control over legally sensitive calculations
Enabled proactive handling of indexation claims
