Project:
Credit Bureau Data Cleanup and Contract Management

Problem:
Debt data must be regularly reported to credit bureaus, but delayed updates can lead to inconsistencies and overlapping calculations in debt operations.
These issues require corrective actions, often involving complex and repetitive manual processes.

Solution:

Designed a Python-based tool to automate preparation of data correction files
Supported two recovery approaches:
Full deletion and re-upload of contract data
Complete credit history reload from the initial event using a custom event logic
Simplified and standardized the correction workflow

Impact:

Reduced manual effort in handling data inconsistencies
Minimized errors in credit bureau reporting
Improved reliability of debt-related calculations
Significantly optimized a previously time-consuming process
