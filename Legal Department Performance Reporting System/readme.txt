Project:
Legal Department Performance Reporting System

Problem:
The legal department required structured reporting to monitor case progress, workload, and overall efficiency.
Manual data aggregation was slow and lacked transparency.

Solution:

Developed complex SQL queries to aggregate and analyze large datasets
Built reporting logic to track legal case workflows and performance metrics
Structured data outputs for easier interpretation and decision-making

Impact:

Enabled data-driven management of legal operations
Improved visibility into case handling and workload distribution
Reduced time spent on manual reporting processes
